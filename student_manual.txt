# Accountancy Capstone Simulation - Student Manual

Welcome to the Accountancy Capstone Simulation! This manual will guide you through completing your assignment.

## Phase 1: Setup & Scenario Generation
1. Launch the application and navigate to **Tab 1: Setup**.
2. Enter your unique **Student ID** (e.g., SIT12345).
3. The system will generate your personalized business case containing 20 transactions for your first month of operations.
4. **Download the COA Template**. This is the strict Chart of Accounts you must use.

## Phase 2: Processing Transactions
1. Open the downloaded `coa_template.csv` in Excel or Google Sheets.
2. Analyze the 20 transactions from Tab 1. Record your journal entries and post them to your Trial Balance template.
3. Ensure that your final Trial Balance balances (Total Debits = Total Credits).
4. Do NOT change the names of the accounts in the template.

## Phase 3: Submission & Audit
1. Navigate to **Tab 2: Submission**.
2. Upload your completed Trial Balance CSV file.
3. Navigate to **Tab 3: Results & Leaderboard** and click **Run Master Auditor**.
4. Review your Score and any specific Pedagogical Feedback indicating areas where your logic may have deviated (especially for TVM or Adjusting entries).

## Phase 4: Leaderboard
- The top 10 most profitable and accurate businesses will be displayed on the Leaderboard.
- Your profit calculation is heavily dependent on correctly recording revenues and expenses.

Good luck!

## Xero Quick Start Guide

### Phase 1: Setup
1. **Sign Up**: Create a free 30-day Xero trial account.
2. **Organization Name**: You MUST name your organization using the format: [Student ID] - [Full Name] Consultancy.
3. **Import COA**: Go to Accounting > Advanced > Chart of Accounts and use the "Import" function with the CSV template downloaded from this portal.

### Phase 2: Recording Entries
1. **Operational**: Record daily sales invoices and bills under Business > Invoices/Bills.
2. **Journal Entries**: For the Loan (TVM) and Adjusting Entries, use Accounting > Reports > Journal Report > Add New Journal.
3. **Note**: Ensure your Jan 31st interest accrual journal entry matches your TVM calculations exactly.

### Phase 3: The Export (Submission)
1. Go to Accounting > Reports > Trial Balance.
2. Select the date: 31 Jan 2026.
3. Click Export > CSV.
4. **Crucial**: Open the CSV and ensure the column headers match the "COA Template" before uploading to the portal.
5. **Verification**: Export the "Detailed Journal Report" from Xero as a PDF. Both files are required for submission.
